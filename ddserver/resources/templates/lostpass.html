Password recovery

You lost your accounts password? Don't panic. You may request a password recovery process using the from on the right. In just a few minutes you will be able to access your account again.

To start the password recovery process, please enter your ddserver username {%- if config.captcha.enabled %} and click the human-check checkbox (which is needed to prevent your account from automatic password resets) {%- endif %}. After you hit the Send button, we will send you an email to the email address specified in your account settings. It will contain further information as well as a password reset code.

If you have any trouble with the password recovery process or you do not receive your recovery code by email in reasonable time, please contact us.
